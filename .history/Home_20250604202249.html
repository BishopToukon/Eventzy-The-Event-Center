About Us

Born from the Beat. Built for the Fans.
Eventzy started with a simple idea: everyone deserves access to amazing live music experiences without the hassle, the hunt, or the heartbreak of missing out.
It all began in 2019 when a group of concert-loving friends kept running into the same problem: missing their favorite shows because they didn’t hear about them in time, getting lost in ticket site chaos, or overpaying for decent seats. So, we decided to fix that.

We built Eventzy to be more than just a ticket platform—it’s your personal concert radar.
From discovering underground gigs to scoring seats at sold-out stadium tours, we’ve got the inside track.
We fuse sleek tech with serious music passion to keep you in the loop, on the list, and at the show.
What started as a passion project has grown into a nationwide hub for fans who live for the next great live moment. With Eventzy, every beat counts—and every fan belongs.
