Refund Policy Notice

Disclaimer:All ticket sales are final. However, we understand that plans can change. To accommodate this, full refunds are available only if a refund request is submitted no later than 7 days before the scheduled concert date. This policy allows us to manage event logistics effectively and ensure that any returned tickets can be redistributed to other fans.

Refund requests submitted within 7 days of the concert—including the day of the event—will not be accepted under any circumstances, except in the case of event cancellation or postponement initiated by the organizers.

To initiate a refund, please contact our support team through the designated refund request form, including your order details and the reason for cancellation. Refunds will be processed within 5–10 business days after approval.

We appreciate your understanding and cooperation. Thank you for supporting live music!
